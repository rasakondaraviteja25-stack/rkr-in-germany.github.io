ABOUT ME

Through my Instagram (rkr_in_germany) and YouTube channel, I have dedicated myself to helping Telugu students navigate the complexities of moving to Germany. My mission is to provide raw, honest, and practical guidance that you won't get from commercial agencies.

Whether it's the admission process, student life tips, or guidance on settling in, I'm here to ensure your transition to Germany is as smooth as possible. Let's build your future together.
